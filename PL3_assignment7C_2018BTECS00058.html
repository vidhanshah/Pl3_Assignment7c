Features of NoSQL Databases

  • NoSQL databases are schema agnostic. You aren’t required to do a lot of up‐front design work before you can store data in NoSQL.
  • It is Nonrelational.
  • NoSQL is highly distributable and uses commodity hardware.

Cricket Statistics

Batsman:
The batsman in a game of cricket is the man who is batting. The batsman rose on his toes and played the rising ball down into the ground. He was the greatest batsman of his generation.
Bowler:
The action of propelling the ball toward the wicket defended by a batter. A player skilled at bowling is called a bowler.
Umpire:
An official who watches a game or match closely to enforce the rules and arbitrate on matters arising from the play.
Bails:
A bail is one of the two smaller sticks placed on top of the three stumps to form a wicket.
Stumps:
The stumps are the three vertical posts that support the bails and form the wicket.
